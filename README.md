📚 Amazon Books Sales and Revenue Analysis


Overview

This project analyzes real-world Amazon Books sales data from a publishing business to identify revenue drivers, category performance, sales trends, and business growth opportunities.
The goal was to convert raw transaction data into actionable business insights using SQL, Excel, Python, and an interactive HTML dashboard.
This project focuses on decision-making, not just reporting — highlighting where revenue comes from, where profit leaks happen, and how business performance can be improved.



Problem Statement
The business needed answers to key questions:

Which products generate the most revenue?
Which categories drive the majority of sales?
What seasonal trends affect demand?
How much revenue is lost to platform charges?
Where can pricing and bundling improve profit?
Which low-performing products should be removed?

Without structured analytics, these decisions were difficult and slow.




Objective
The main objectives were to:

Analyze sales and net revenue performance
Identify top-performing categories and products
Measure Amazon platform fee impact
Detect seasonality and operational trends
Build pricing and bundle simulations
Recommend growth strategies using data

Key Insights

1. Strong Revenue Performance
Total Gross Revenue reached ₹1.26 Cr
Net Revenue after Amazon charges: ₹90.7L

Insight: healthy topline but significant platform dependency.




2. Platform Fee Impact
Amazon retained 28.1% of revenue.

Insight: small reduction in platform cost could significantly improve profit.




3. Revenue Concentration Risk
Top 2 categories generated 92.4% of revenue.

Insight: over-dependence creates business risk.




4. Seasonal Demand Exists
Q1 and Q3 performed strongest.
Peak months linked to exam cycles.

Insight: strong seasonal business pattern.




5. Daily Revenue Spikes
Peak day revenue: ₹41,075

Insight: event-driven demand spikes should be planned for.




6. Product Portfolio Imbalance
Bottom 20% contributes very little.

Insight: product rationalization opportunity.




7. Pricing Opportunity
Price simulation suggests additional revenue potential.

Insight: current pricing may be conservative.




8. Bundle Opportunity
Frequently purchased titles indicate upsell potential.

Insight: bundle strategy can improve average order value.





Business Recommendations
Revenue
Reduce platform dependency where possible.
Pricing

Marketing

Inventory

Sales Growth
